The Magifind Story

Magifind was founded by Thomas van Turnhout and Koen van Eijk, two lifelong friends with a shared passion for innovation. After successful corporate careers and the establishment of their first venture, Oakwood Technologies, they embarked on a new journey to transform the search landscape.

With their groundbreaking Smart Robotic Process Automation software suite, Automagica, and other solutions Thomas and Koen served high-profile companies such as Google, Adidas, KPMG, VRT, Sporza, LiveNation, and Maria Middelares hospital. Their success led to the acquisition of Oakwood Technologies by Netcall plc, where they continued to drive innovation for another three years.

Magifind: The AI-Powered Search Revolution

Magifind is a semantic search engine that delivers unparalleled search experiences. Our autonomous crawling technology seamlessly gathers content and metadata from websites, enabling swift integration within minutes. Unlike competitors that require costly custom integrations, Magifind offers a full-service, end-to-end solution for exceptional search functionality on your website.

Our Values and Culture

At Magifind, we are committed to open source and transparency. We believe in sharing our journey and insights through our blog, fostering a culture of collaboration and knowledge-sharing. Our team is driven by a passion for innovation and a dedication to delivering cutting-edge solutions that make a real difference in people's lives.

Although Magifind is a young company, our founders bring a wealth of experience and a proven track record of success.
